A Comprehensive Analysis of Data Professionals Survey

Being interested in the field of data, I was immediately drawn to this dataset consisting of survey responses from individuals who work with data. The objective of this project is to analyze our sample based on factors such as gender and age, and to gain valuable insights into data professionals' perspectives on their salaries, job satisfaction, and opinions about the industry.

Seattle AirBnB Analysis: Unlocking Insights for Hosts

The client has two properties in Seattle and wants to determine the optimal listing strategy for each property on Airbnb. This project aims to analyse the distribution of Airbnb listings in Seattle, including pricing and property characteristics, to provide insights on when and which property to list on Airbnb.

The Effect of COVID-19 and the Significance of Vaccination

The goal of this project is to assess the impact of COVID-19 globally, with a specific focus on Portugal, and to investigate the role of vaccination in reducing COVID-19 deaths. To achieve this goal, the project will analyze data on COVID-19 cases and deaths worldwide, and in Portugal specifically, over time. The project will also analyze data on vaccination rates in Portugal and investigate the relationship between vaccination and COVID-19 deaths.

Evolution of Netflix contents (2016-2021)

Netflix is an American subscription video on-demand streaming service and production company. The main goal is to find out how Netflix’s content changed over time (2016-2021) and what content Netflix has available based on rating and category.

A Wine Enthusiast’s perspective on Portuguese wine

Find a different perspective on Portuguese wine (is Portuguese wine known, have high quality and cheap price?, what provinces are most well-known?, what varieties the wine tasters are most fond of?), as well new suggestions of high quality wine that is not expensive.
